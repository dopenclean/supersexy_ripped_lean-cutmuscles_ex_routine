## PROGRESSION SYSTEM

### **Weeks 1-4: Foundation (Learn the movements)**
- Focus: Perfect form
- Tempo: 3-0-1 (3 seconds down, no pause, 1 second up)
- Rest: 90 seconds on big lifts, 60 seconds on accessories
- Goal: Establish baseline. Track what weight you can handle.

### **Weeks 5-8: Muscle Building**
- Tempo: 4-0-1 (4 second negatives = more muscle damage)
- When you hit top of rep range (e.g., 12 reps), add 1-2kg next session
- Goal: Build muscle mass while maintaining form

### **Weeks 9-12: Refinement**
- Tempo: 3-1-1 (add 1 second pause at hardest position)
- This eliminates momentum = pure strength
- You should be 2-4kg stronger than Week 1 on major lifts

### **Weeks 13-16: Density (More work, less rest)**
- Tempo: 3-1-1 maintained  
- Rest: Reduce to 60 seconds on everything
- More work in less time = better conditioning + fat loss
- If strength drops >15%, add rest back

### **Every 5th Week: DELOAD (Recovery week)**
- Cut total sets by 50%
- Keep same weights
- Use easy tempo (2-0-1)
- Focus on sleep, mobility, recovery
- This prevents burnout and injury

### **1. Progressive Overload**
- Every week, aim to do slightly more than last week

- Get to 12-15% body fat (this is NON-NEGOTIABLE)
