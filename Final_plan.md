## üéØ PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD STRATEGY

### Month 1: Master Form & Build Base
- **Goal:** Perfect technique, establish baseline strength
- **Tempo:** 3-0-1 standard
- **Rest:** Full rest periods (60-90 sec)
- **Track:** Write down all weights and reps completed

### Month 2: Add Weight
- **Goal:** Increase load when hitting top of rep range
- **Example:** If you hit 4√ó12 reps, add 1-2kg next session
- **Tempo:** Keep 3-0-1
- **Track:** Every time you increase weight

### Month 3: Time Under Tension
- **Goal:** Build muscle with slower negatives
- **Tempo:** 4-0-1 (4 seconds down, explode up)
- **Load:** May need to reduce weight by 2-3kg
- **Benefit:** More muscle fiber recruitment

### Month 4: Density Phase
- **Goal:** More work in less time
- **Tempo:** Back to 3-0-1
- **Rest:** Reduce to 60 seconds (from 90)
- **Challenge:** Same weight, less rest = conditioning

### Every 4th Week: DELOAD (Mandatory)
- Cut all sets by 50% (keep exercises the same)
- Use same weights
- Faster tempo (2-0-1)
- Extra sleep and recovery
- This prevents burnout and injury
